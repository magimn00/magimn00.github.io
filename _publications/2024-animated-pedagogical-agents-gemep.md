This paper investigates whether people can recognize emotions expressed by animated pedagogical agents through affective gestures extracted from the GEMEP dataset. The study focuses on how arm and hand gestures can support emotional expression in animated agents.

The work was published in the ArtsIT 2023 proceedings as part of Springer’s Lecture Notes of the Institute for Computer Sciences, Social Informatics and Telecommunications Engineering.
